What is Bits For Work?

Bits For Work is an online exchange for individuals and freelancers to exchange their servies and skills for bitcoins and other cryptocurrencies.

We're here to kickstart the Bitcoin revolution by giving you a market to trade your Bitcoins for valuable services.

At BFW, we don't force a maximum price on services since we believe in a fair pay for a persons work. You set the price!

Why Bitcoins?

Our goal is to connect people from around the world to create a truly global exchange.

Bitcoin is the only currency that makes sense for the globally connected world that we live in.

With Bitcoins you don't have to worry about paying extraordinary fees or transferring currencies.

FAQ

How does it work?
Freelancers can post services and jobs that they are willing to do and the price for that unit of work. Multiple units of work can be purchased at the same time so there is no set maximum price.
Is there a minimum price?
Yes, the minimum price is 0.05B⃦ and goes up in mulitples from that.
Do you charge a fee?
Yes we do charge a fee, however it is relatively small at only 5%.
Can I use other Cryptocurrencies?
Yes, we are developing the site to accept multiple cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin and Litecoin among others.
